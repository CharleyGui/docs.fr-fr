 <span data-ttu-id="ecc95-104">Une déclaration d’ [interface](../language-reference/keywords/interface.md) explicite a été trouvée en dehors d’une [classe](../language-reference/keywords/class.md) ou d’une [structure](../language-reference/keywords/struct.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ecc95-104">An explicit [interface](../language-reference/keywords/interface.md) declaration was found outside a [class](../language-reference/keywords/class.md) or [struct](../language-reference/keywords/struct.md).</span></span>
